Buggy design rules

Flag design:
  1. For every pattern except plain, the secondary colour must be different from the original colour.
Wheels and tyres:
  1. The number of wheels on your buggy must be even.
  2. The number of tyres must be equal to or greater than the number of wheels.
Power and backup power:
  1. You can only have one unit of non-consumbale power (non-consumable powers: Fusion reactor, Thermonuclear reactor, Bank of solar panels, Sailing rig) per motive force.
Hamster booster:
  1. Only effective if you have hamster motive power.
Armour:
  1. Armour is not a requirement for entry in any race.
Attack:
  1. Each attack can be deployed once and only once.
  2. The likelihood of being able to execute the attack is influenced by your buggy's race computer algorithm.
Algorithm:
  1. Must not be "buggy". Buggy is what happens when your race computer goes wrong: it is not a state you choose.
